Responsiveness Testing

Users: millennials/genY who rely heavily on internet. they will browse and purchase their dog on this one site.

Analytics: Desktops remain important in design and still takes up most usage in the shopping category. (And for the sake of research adopting a dog will be considered a purchase.) However, millennials do 73% of their shopping online. Additionally, it was important to note that a great number of millennials use Apple products.
  Sources: https://www.stonetemple.com/mobile-vs-desktop-usage-study/, https://www.adweek.com/digital/infographic-73-of-millennials-use-their-phones-to-shop-online/

Design Method: graceful degradation

Website testing was performed on several web browsers and devices based on analytics and user profiles.
